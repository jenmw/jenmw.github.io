Arithmagic Landing Page

‘Arithmagic’ is a children’s game in the fantasy genre that reinforces mathematical operations with quick-fire questions. I worked on designs for the landing page, but the assets and the game itself were made by very talented coworkers of mine. It was a pleasure to create the landing page to highlight their work.

Initial UI sketches and page layout sketch.

Here I’ve pulled a selection of my personal favourite assets to work on. With the art style being quite simple and making use of block colour vectors it was really important to keep pushing for stronger contrasting colour pairings to make those edges really stand out. The vine dividers tile smoothly together by design. The leaderboard presented a really interesting challenge, as there are a lot of different ways I could have presented that information, but I’m really pleased with the top three podium layout I ended up going for. And finally, choosing much bolder colours on the CTA buttons proved really effective, as you can see on the full layout below.
